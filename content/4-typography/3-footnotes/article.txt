Title: Footnotes

----

Headline: 

----

Text: 

Every good book needs a little footnote here and there.[^1] A footnote is made of two things: a marker in the text that will become a superscript number and a footnote definition that will be placed in a list of footnotes at the end of the document. A footnote looks like this:[^2]

```
Every good book needs a little footnote here and there.[^1]

[^1]: Yes, it most certainly does!
```

[^1]: Yes, it most certainly does!
[^2]: It's as easy as that.
